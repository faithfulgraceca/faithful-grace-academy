A Community Built on Faith

Faithful Grace Christian Academy is a 2nd–12th grade Christian school offering individualized, mastery-based education through the Accelerated Christian Education (A.C.E.) curriculum. Our Christ-centered approach integrates biblical principles, character development, and academic rigor across every subject — ensuring students grow in knowledge, faith, and personal integrity.

We are committed to meeting each student exactly where they are. Through diagnostic placement, phonetic-based reading instruction, and a dedicated 7:1 student-to-teacher ratio, our staff tailors every learning plan to the unique needs and pace of each child — preparing them not only for academic success, but for a life of purpose and faithful service.

Expanding to Kindergarten — "Learning to Read" Program Coming Soon

Faithful Grace Christian Academy is actively developing a Kindergarten and phonics-based "Learning to Read" program.
